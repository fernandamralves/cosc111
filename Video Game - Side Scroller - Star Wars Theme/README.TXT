PROJECT TITLE: SideScroller - The Rebel Alliance
PURPOSE OF PROJECT: Lab developed for COSC 111
VERSION or DATE: 1.0 - released 2 December 2021
HOW TO START THIS PROJECT: hit run on your IDE
AUTHORS: Fernanda Malheiros Rodrigues Alves
USER INSTRUCTIONS: your goal is to make as many points as possible.
	You are part of the Alliance and need to kill as many Stormtroopers as possible and collect Droids.
	You lose points by killing droids or Yoda. You gain life by collecting Yodas.
	Arrow right: laser
	Arrow up: move up
	Arrow down: move down
